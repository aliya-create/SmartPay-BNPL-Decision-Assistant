The best plan is not always the plan with the lowest monthly payment.
The product insight

A shorter plan may reduce the duration of a commitment but create higher short-term payment concentration.


A longer plan may reduce the immediate monthly impact while extending the customer's repayment timeline.


The right product experience should make this tradeoff visible before the customer commits.

```
```
Product evolution

From payment option to payment context.

01 · VISIBILITY

Show the calendar

Make the impact of each plan visible alongside existing commitments.

02 · COMPARISON

Show the tradeoff

Help customers understand the difference between monthly impact and repayment duration.

03 · ASSISTANCE

Recommend with context

Use payment history, affordability signals, and due-date logic to explain a best-fit option.

04 · ANTICIPATION

Think ahead

Eventually help customers understand how future commitments could affect their payment calendar.
